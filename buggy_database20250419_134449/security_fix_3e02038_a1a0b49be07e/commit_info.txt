f2fs: support fault injection for f2fs_trylock_op()

This patch supports to inject fault into f2fs_trylock_op().

Usage:
a) echo 65536 > /sys/fs/f2fs/<dev>/inject_type or
b) mount -o fault_type=65536 <dev> <mountpoint>
